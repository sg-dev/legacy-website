«Review

As a service to the scientific community, we write quite a number of reviews on submissions for journals and conferences. This activity involves all members of our team and is supervised by Frank Schweitzer. Certainly, it is very time consuming, but it also helps to learn about the strengths and weaknesses in scientific publications and gives a good overview of recent research trends.
